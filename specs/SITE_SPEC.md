## Design Direction

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep lapis blue (#1B3A6B) — Afghanistan is the world's historic source of lapis lazuli
- Accent: Warm amber/gold (#D4891A) — saffron, traditional textiles
- Background: Off-white (#F8F5EF)
- Text: Near-black (#1A1A1A)
- Success/CTA hover: Teal (#2A7A6F)

**Typography:**
- Headings: A bold serif (e.g., Playfair Display) — gravitas, heritage feel
- Body: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Source Sans Pro)
- Dari/Farsi text: Noto Naskh Arabic or Vazirmatn

**Photography Style:**
- Expansive landscape shots with high saturation
- Authentic people photography (with permission)
- Avoid stock photo clichés — real images from actual trips only

**Tone of Voice:**
- Confident, warm, honest
- Does not downplay challenges (safety) but reframes them positively
- First-person from Ali Reza where appropriate ("I've guided travelers here for 15 years...")

## Technical Requirements

- **Platform:** WordPress + Divi or Webflow (client-editable)
- **Languages:** English + Dari (RTL support required)
- **Forms:** Connected to email + WhatsApp notification
- **SEO:** Meta titles/descriptions per page, schema markup for tours (TouristAttraction, TouristTrip)
- **Speed:** Images served via CDN, lazy loading
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4
- **Booking:** Simple inquiry-based (no live payment processing initially); Stripe integration optional Phase 2
- **SSL:** Required
- **Mobile:** Fully responsive, mobile-first design
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance target
