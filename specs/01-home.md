**Section heading:** Why Ali Reza Travel

Ali Reza was born and raised in Afghanistan. He knows the back roads, the community leaders, and the places that don't appear in any guidebook.

**Safety First**
Every route is planned against current ground conditions. If something changes, Ali Reza changes the plan — and he communicates that to you in real time.

No tourist infrastructure, no staged experiences. Homestays, local guides, meals cooked by families who rarely host foreigners.
